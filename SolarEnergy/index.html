WHO WE ARE

More than 20 years ago, Solar sold some of the very first solar panels to the public out of a small general store in Almaty.


Solar energy is one of the new types of energy production based on renewable sources, in particular, on solar energy. The main goal is to convert solar radiation into other technological types of energy used by humans for their own needs. This type of energy is inexhaustible and can be considered potentially as an energy resource that can change modern ideas about energy supply and fully meet the needs of humanity.
